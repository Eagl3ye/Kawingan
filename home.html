ARTIST OF THE DAY

ILLENIUM

      Nick Miller A.K.A Illenium is well-known for his future bass tracks along with his acoustic guitar intro and good collaborations with the different known and amateur vocalists. He became popular in the EDM Industry after the release of his first album "Ashes" and this year he gained more fans after the release of his second album "Awake". Illenium's album "Ashes" became sixth on the Billboard Dance/Electronic Albums chart, and nineteenth on the Billboard Top Heatseekers chart.
      He had a distinct, a signature vibe added on each and every EDM track he made and it is not the same as the mainstream EDM Future Bass you have ever heard of before. He had been also complimented by his fans and collaborators for utilizing the vocals ethically and with respect with the collaborators and still retaining the good quality of the vocal samples. He had been touring around the world lately and still pursuing his career as an EDM artist.
